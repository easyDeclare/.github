## About easyDeclare

**easyDeclare** builds on the declarative process modeling language [Declare](https://www.win.tue.nl/declare/) and proposes a new graphical notation designed according to the *Physics of Notations (PoN)* principles by Moody.
The notation has been validated through expert interviews and controlled experiments. The *easyDeclare Designer (EDD)* tool enables direct modeling using this notation.

Find out more about easyDeclare in our [research paper](https://github.com/easyDeclare/paper-artifacts/blob/main/EasyDeclare__Camera_Ready_.pdf).
